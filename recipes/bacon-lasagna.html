Bacon Lasagna

What you will need:

  • 24 uncooked lasagna noodles;
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef (90% lean);
  • 2 medium onions, chopped;
  • 1-1/2 pounds bacon strips, cooked and crumbled;
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce;
  • 2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) diced tomatoes, undrained;
  • 2 tablespoons sugar;
  • 1 tablespoon salt;
  • 8 cups shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese.

How to cook it:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F;
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions for al dente. Drain;
  3. In a 6-qt stockpot, cook beef and onions over medium-high heat until beef is no longer pink, 10-12 minutes, breaking beef into crumbles. Drain;
  4. Stir in bacon, tomato sauce, tomatoes, sugar and salt. Heat through;
  5. Spread 1 cup sauce into each of 2 greased 13x9-in. Backing dishes. Layer each with 4 noodles, overlapping as needed, 1-2/3 cups sauce and 1-1/3 cups cheese. Repeat layers twice;
  6. Bake, covered, for 40 minutes;
  7. Uncover. Bake until bubbly, 5-10 minutes longer;
  8. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.

Prep Time:

20 minutes

Cook Time:

50 minutes

Servings:

5 servings
