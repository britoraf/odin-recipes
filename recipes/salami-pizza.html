Salami Pizza

Or Pizza al salame like in Italy

Feel yourself in Italy with this round tasteful thing.

(personally I do not like to share with other people. One entire only for me is enough).

What you will need:

    Dough:

  • 200g manitoba flour;
  • 270g pastry flour;
  • 30g durum wheat semolina;
  • 300ml water lukewarm;
  • 35ml olive oil extra virgin;
  • 10g salt;
  • 5g fresh yeast.

    • Tomato sauce:

  • 500g San Marzano tomatoes passed;
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano;
  • 1 tablespoon salt;
  • 1 tablespoon pepper;
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil extra virgin;

    • Topping:

  • 2 balls mozzarella cheese;
  • 10g salami finely sliced;
  • Dried oregano

How to cook it:

    Dough

  1. Add the yeast to the lukewarm water and dissolve. Put the wheat flour, 00 flour, durum wheat semolina and salt in the mixing bowl of the food processor and let knead with the dough hook on the lowest speed.;
  2. Gradually add the yeast water. Then add the oil as well. Let the dough knead for at least 7 minutes. The longer, the better.;
  3. Once the dough has a nice smooth surface and there are no more dough scraps hanging from the mixing bowl, turn off the machine. Release the dough from the hook, shape it into a ball and return it to the bowl. Cover with a cloth and let rest for at least 2 hours.;
  4. Tip: The longer the dough can rest, the better it will be! It is best to prepare the pizza dough a day in advance and then let it rest in the refrigerator, covered with a cloth. Then take the dough out about 3 hours before preparation and let it rest covered at room temperature. After resting, transfer the dough to a floured work surface, divide into pieces of about 250g and form each into balls. Cover everything again with a cloth and let rest for another 30 minutes;

    Tomato sauce

  1. Put the strained tomatoes, salt, pepper, olive oil and oregano in a bowl and mix thoroughly;

    Topping

  1. Preheat the oven to 250째C (500째F) top/bottom heat;
  2. Fray the mozzarella into small pieces with your hands or cut into slices.
  3. Roll out each piece of dough on a floured work surface and then transfer to a baking sheet. Pour some of the tomato sauce on top and spread evenly. Leave about 1cm of the edge free. Top generously with mozzarella. Then spread the finely sliced salami on top;
  4. Then bake for about 10 minutes at 250째C (500째F) top/bottom heat on the lowest rack;
  5. Remove the pizza from the oven after baking, sprinkle with a little oregano, serve immediately and enjoy hot;
  6. Buon appetito!

30 minutes

Servings:

2 servings
