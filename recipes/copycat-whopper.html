Copycat Burger King Whopper

This beast will taste like a real Burger King Whopper

And the better: It will cost almost the half of a real one.

(but tasting better, despite his ugliness).

What you will need:

  • 2 Impossible burger patties
  • 2 burger buns
  • 1/4 cup sliced white onion
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 6 pickle slices
  • 1 beefsteak tomato
  • 2 large leaves of curly leaf lettuce

How to cook it:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F;
  2. If your burgers are not thin patties, flatten them out with a rolling pin sandwiching them between two sheets of parchment paper;
  3. Place the burger patties on a foil-lined baking tray and cook as per cooking instructions on the packaging;
  4. Slice the white onion into thin discs;
  5. Once the burger patties are done, place the patties onto the bottom burger bun;
  6. Top each burger patty with 1 tablespoon ketchup, 1 tablespoon mayonnaise, 2 discs of white onion, 3 slices of pickle, 2 slices of beefsteak tomato and 1 large leaf of curly lettuce. Repeat for the second burger;
  7. Cover the burgers with the top part of the bun and destroy and sent it to the void of your stomach.

10 minutes

Cook Time:

20 minutes

2 servings
